About me

Hi, my name is Amanda.

I am a data visualization designer who shapes data into stories. As someone who comes from an academic background, my passion is to bring dull and arcane datasets to life in exciting and dynamic ways. From story ideation to research and analysis, all the way to development, my goal is to create intuitive and beautiful visualizations for you to (1) enjoy and (2) learn something new from!

Based out of Brooklyn, I'm a recent graduate of data journalism at the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism and looking for new opportunities to exercise my data and design skills. When I'm not tinkering on my laptop, I'm probably watching a movie, or people-watching at a park. Currently, I'm learning front-end web development to build interactive stories and miscrosites like this one.

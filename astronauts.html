How hard is it to become an astronaut anyway?

Time frame: 2 weeks

​​​​​​​Tools used: Adobe XD, Illustrator, Scrollama.js, PIXI.js, D3.js, HTML/CSS

Role: Designer, developer, and writer

About

A project for the Lede program 2022 - an intensive data journalism course at Columbia's J School.

Turns out, it is 62 times harder to become an astronaut than it is to get into Harvard. This project visualizes the application journey that 12,000 candidates take to become one of NASA's Artemis Generation of astronauts—set to go to the moon by 2024.

A Day in the Life

Time frame: 1 week

​​​​​​​Tools used: Adobe Illustrator

Role: Designer

About

A personal project by Amanda Chen.

This project was inspired by Nathan Yau's A Day in the Life of Americans.

My goal for this project was to visualize what the average American is doing in a typical day as a static, infographic. I wanted to experiment with a Nightingale Rose chart since it's cyclical form, I thought, represents the routine of day-to-day life well.

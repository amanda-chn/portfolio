Dear Lede

Time frame: 1 week

​​​​​​​Tools used: Pen and paper, Adobe Illustrator, HTML/CSS

Role: Designer, developer, and writer

About

A project for the Lede program 2022 - an intensive data journalism course at Columbia's J School.

This is a collection of data visualizations on postcards, inspired by Dear Data. During my time at Lede, I hand-collected data on parameters I thought would be interesting to reflect on after graduation. From how many hours I spent learning new tools, to what my weekly schedule looked like, this is a visual exploration of my time as a Lede student and a letter of lessons learned to future cohorts.
